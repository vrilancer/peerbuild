About PeerBuild

PeerBuild exists to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world execution by helping biology students overcome coding anxiety through practical, project-first learning.

Traditional university courses and massive online platforms focus heavily on passive lectures, memorization, and generic certificates that do not prove actual capability.

PeerBuild changes that by focusing on action over theory. Students immediately start building functional scripts to analyze real biological datasets.

No false promises. No paper certificates. Just real projects, real debugging, and real GitHub proof of work.

Built by a student, for students. Honest. Practical. Action-oriented.
